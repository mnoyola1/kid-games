## Visual Style Guidelines for Lumina Racer

### Core Aesthetic:
**Modern Racing Game Quality** - Think Wipeout, F-Zero GX, or TrackMania with a magical twist. Professional 3D-rendered or high-quality 2D digital art. NO pixel art.

### Key Visual Elements:

1. **Color Scheme**: 
   - Primary: Neon cyan, electric blue, vibrant purple, hot pink
   - Accents: Gold, white, orange (for speed)
   - Magic effects: Purple, blue, teal with sparkle particles
   - High contrast for visibility at speed

2. **Materials & Surfaces**:
   - Sleek metallic finishes (chrome, brushed metal, iridescent)
   - Holographic interfaces and displays
   - Translucent energy effects
   - Reflective surfaces with dynamic lighting
   - Glowing neon accents everywhere

3. **Speed & Motion**:
   - Always include motion blur on fast-moving elements
   - Particle trails and energy streams
   - Light trails following vehicles
   - Dynamic camera angles suggesting velocity
   - Sense of speed in every asset

4. **Tech-Fantasy Fusion**:
   - Blend advanced sci-fi technology with magical fantasy elements
   - Circuit patterns that look like magical runes
   - Holographic displays with mystical symbols
   - Energy effects that feel both technological and magical
   - Modern and timeless simultaneously

5. **Atmosphere**:
   - Exciting and energetic, never dull
   - Positive and encouraging tone
   - Professional polish and quality
   - Kid-friendly but not childish
   - Appealing to players of all ages

6. **Readability**:
   - UI must be instantly readable at high speeds
   - High contrast between UI and gameplay
   - Clear iconography and symbols
   - Bold, easily readable fonts
   - Color coding for quick recognition

7. **Consistency**:
   - Maintain unified art style across all assets
   - Vehicles should look like they belong in same universe
   - Consistent lighting direction (usually from above/front)
   - Matching material quality and detail level
   - Coherent color palette throughout

### Technical Specifications:

- **Resolution**: High resolution for modern displays (1920x1080 minimum, 2560x1440 preferred)
- **Format**: PNG with transparency for sprites/UI, JPG for backgrounds acceptable
- **Color Space**: RGB, vibrant and saturated colors
- **Lighting**: Dramatic with strong highlights and colored lights
- **Effects**: Particle systems, glow effects, motion blur, depth of field

### Reference Games for Visual Quality:
- Wipeout Omega Collection (sleek futuristic racing)
- F-Zero GX (high-speed intense racing aesthetic)
- TrackMania (colorful, exciting tracks)
- Mario Kart 8 (polished, professional character design)
- Rocket League (clean UI and vehicle design)

### Animation Requirements:

**For Sprite Sheets (if needed):**
- Each animation frame in separate file
- Consistent character/vehicle size and position
- Smooth transitions between frames
- 30-60fps animation quality

**Vehicle States:**
1. Idle hover (floating with subtle bobbing)
2. Acceleration (forward lean with engine glow)
3. Left turn (banking left with drift trail)
4. Right turn (banking right with drift trail)
5. Boost activation (intense energy burst)
6. Victory crossing (celebratory pose)

**Aurora States:**
1. Idle floating (gentle hovering animation)
2. Cheering (excited jump with sparkles)
3. Encouraging (motivating gesture)
4. Celebrating victory (triumphant pose)
5. Sympathetic support (gentle "try again" expression)

### Quality Checklist:

Before finalizing any Lumina Racer asset:
- ✅ Modern 3D-rendered or high-quality 2D digital art style
- ✅ NO pixel art or retro aesthetics
- ✅ Professional racing game quality
- ✅ Appropriate resolution (1024px+ for characters/vehicles)
- ✅ Dramatic lighting with neon accents
- ✅ Motion blur or speed effects where appropriate
- ✅ Tech-fantasy aesthetic maintained
- ✅ Instantly recognizable and readable
- ✅ Exciting and energetic feel
- ✅ Transparent backgrounds for sprites/UI
- ✅ Consistent with other Lumina Racer assets

---

## Pro Tips for Generation:

1. **Be Specific About Style**: Always mention "professional racing game quality" and "NOT pixel art"
2. **Reference Modern Games**: Mention Wipeout, F-Zero, or similar in prompts
3. **Emphasize Effects**: Call out specific effects like glow, motion blur, particles
4. **Request Multiple Angles**: For vehicles, generate front, side, and 3/4 views separately
5. **Iterate on Quality**: Generate 3-5 versions and pick the best or composite elements
6. **Test at Speed**: View assets while scrolling quickly to ensure they read well in motion
7. **Maintain Consistency**: Use the same descriptive terms across all related assets

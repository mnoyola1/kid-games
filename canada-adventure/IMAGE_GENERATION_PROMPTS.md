# Canada Adventure - Image Generation Prompts for Gemini

## Game Overview
Educational RPG where players explore 7 regions of Canada, battling monsters by answering geography questions. Each region has unique themes, enemies, and visual styles representing different parts of Canada.

## Tips for Canadian Cultural Authenticity:

1. **Colors**: Use red and white (Canadian flag) as accent colors
2. **Symbols**: Maple leaf should appear throughout
3. **Regional Accuracy**: Research actual regional characteristics
4. **Educational Value**: Make landmarks and geography recognizable
5. **Respectful Design**: Honor Indigenous and cultural elements appropriately
6. **Kid-Friendly**: Educational without being scary or inappropriate

## Character Animation Frames:

## Tips for Word Forge Visual Style:

1. **Color Temperature**: Warm forge colors (oranges, reds) with magical cool accents (blues, purples)
2. **Progression Visual**: Items should clearly show quality increase from common to legendary
3. **Particle Effects**: Constant ember particles create forge atmosphere
4. **Metallic Shaders**: Items should have clear metallic/material properties
5. **Lighting**: Strong light sources from forge fire and magical runes
6. **Fantasy Elements**: Balance medieval realism with magical fantasy

## Rarity Color Codes:
- **Common**: Gray (#808080)
- **Uncommon**: Green (#00FF00)
- **Rare**: Blue (#0000FF)
- **Epic**: Purple (#9900FF)
- **Legendary**: Gold (#FFD700)

Use these colors consistently for borders, glows, and rarity indicators.

## Animation Sequences Needed:

### Crafting Process:
1. Place materials on anvil
2. Hammer strike with sparks
3. Magical energy imbue
4. Item reveal with rarity glow
5. Success celebration

### Ingredient Collection:
1. Word spelled correctly
2. Material appears with glow
3. Flies to inventory
4. Counter increases

Generate each frame separately maintaining consistent visual style.

## 1. SPELL SIEGE üè∞

**Genre:** Tower Defense + Spelling  
**Target Players:** Liam (3rd grade) & Emma (5th grade)  
**Duration:** 20-30 minutes per session

### Core Gameplay
Players defend their castle from waves of enemies by typing/spelling words correctly. Each correctly spelled word damages enemies, while mistakes let them get closer to the castle. The game features dynamic difficulty scaling and progressive wave complexity.

### Key Features
- **Two Difficulty Modes:**
  - **Liam Mode:** Contraction words (won't, aren't, they're), slower pace, 3 max enemies, 6 starting health
  - **Emma Mode:** Fantasy words (dragon, crystal, quest), faster pace, 5 max enemies, 5 starting health
- **Enemy Types:**
  - Goblin (1 hit, 10 coins)
  - Knight (2 hits, 25 coins)
  - Demon (3 hits, 50 coins)
- **Upgrade System:**
  - Spell Power (+1 damage per hit)
  - Slow Field (enemies move slower)
  - Shield (block one hit)
  - Castle Repair (restore 1 heart)
- **Music Integration:** Full soundtrack with 7 tracks (menu, early/mid/final gameplay, boss, victory, game over)

### Educational Value
- Spelling practice with grade-appropriate vocabulary
- Speed and accuracy under pressure
- Strategic resource management
- Pattern recognition (enemy spawning)

### Progression System
- Wave-based progression (1-10+)
- Dynamic speed increase per wave
- Boss encounters at key waves
- High score and high wave tracking

**Genre:** Educational RPG + Geography Quiz  
**Target Players:** Emma (5th grade study material)  
**Duration:** 15-20 minutes per session

  3. **Ontario** (Level 3) - City Slime & Tower Bot

  5. **Plains** (Level 5) - Wheat Wraith & Tornado Spirit
  6. **Rockies** (Level 6) - Mountain Yeti & Rock Golem
  7. **Arctic** (Level 7) - Ice Dragon & Blizzard Witch
- **Pixel Art Sprites:** Custom character and monster designs
- **Battle System:**
  - Answer questions correctly to deal damage
  - Wrong answers = take damage
  - Combo system (up to 5x multiplier)
  - Health management strategy

### Educational Value
- Canadian geography facts (33 questions)
- History (explorers, Seven Years' War, confederation)
- Economics (trade, industries, resources)
- Vocabulary (prairie, tundra, lowland, province, barter)
- Indigenous peoples (Inuit, First Nations)

### Question Topics
- Physical geography (oceans, mountains, plains)
- Cities and population
- Historical events and figures
- Government and economy
- Animals and natural resources

### Progression System
- Progressive region unlocking
- Increasing difficulty per region
- Boss battles for each region
- Achievement tracking (50 questions answered, all regions unlocked)

## 4. DUNGEON FORGE ‚öíÔ∏èüó°Ô∏è

**Genre:** Roguelike + Spelling Combat + Dungeon Crawler  
**Target Players:** Emma & Liam  
**Duration:** 15-30 minutes per run

### Core Gameplay
A roguelike dungeon crawler where spelling words correctly is your combat mechanic! Explore procedurally generated dungeons, battle monsters by spelling words, craft powerful items at forges, and collect permanent upgrades. Each run is unique, and death means starting over - but you keep meta-progression fragments to unlock permanent upgrades.

### Key Features
- **Procedural Dungeon Generation:**
  - Each floor has 8-12 randomly generated rooms
  - Room types: Combat, Forge, Treasure, Boss
  - Multiple paths through each floor
  - Increasing difficulty per floor

- **Spelling-Based Combat:**
  - Spell words correctly to attack enemies
  - Wrong spelling = enemy attacks you
  - Combo system rewards perfect spelling
  - Floor difficulty scales word complexity

- **9 Craftable Items with Real Purpose:**
  - Weapons (Iron Sword, Flame Blade, Thunder Hammer, Dragon Slayer)
  - Armor (Leather Vest, Steel Armor, Phoenix Armor)
  - Accessories (Health Ring, Healing Potion)
  - Each item has stats that affect combat
  - Items unlock special abilities triggered by spelling specific words

- **5 Enemy Types + Boss:**
  - Slime (weak, easy words)
  - Goblin (medium threat)
  - Skeleton (harder words required)
  - Fire Elemental (advanced spelling)
  - Ancient Dragon (epic boss battle)

- **Permadeath + Meta Progression:**
  - Death ends the run
  - Keep "fragments" earned from defeating enemies
  - Spend fragments on permanent upgrades
  - Upgrades: Max Health, Starting Items, Extra Life

- **Strategic Depth:**
  - Manage HP between combats
  - Choose which rooms to enter
  - Equip best items for your playstyle
  - Risk vs reward decisions (skip forge? Fight extra enemies?)

### Educational Value
- Spelling under pressure (combat urgency)
- Progressive difficulty (easy ‚Üí medium ‚Üí hard words)
- Vocabulary expansion (fantasy/dungeon terms)
- Strategic planning (resource management)
- Persistence & learning from failure (roguelike design)

### Progression System
- **Per-Run Progress:**
  - Collect items during exploration
  - Defeat enemies for XP and coins
  - Clear rooms to advance
  - Defeat floor boss to descend deeper

- **Meta Progression (Between Runs):**
  - Earn fragments from all runs (even failed ones)
  - Unlock permanent upgrades
  - Track furthest floor reached
  - Leaderboard for deepest delve

## 8. SHADOWS IN THE HALLS üî¶

**Genre:** Survival Horror + Educational Puzzles  
**Target Players:** Emma (10, brave) & Liam (8, with parent support)  
**Duration:** 20-30 minutes per run (roguelike)

### Core Gameplay
Top-down survival horror where players explore a dark, procedurally-generated school building at night. They must manage their flashlight battery, avoid shadow enemies, and solve educational puzzles to progress and escape.

### Key Features
- **Battery Management System:**
  - Drains 1% per second (100s full battery)
  - Drains 2% per second when running
  - Low battery (20%) reduces light radius
  - Battery pickups restore 60%
- **Flashlight Mechanics:**
  - Normal radius: 150px
  - Low battery radius: 75px
  - Light reveals safe path
  - Darkness hides dangers
- **Procedural Generation:**
  - 5√ó5 grid of rooms
  - 7 room types (hallway, classroom, office, bathroom, safe room, library, cafeteria)
  - Random item placement
  - Random enemy spawns
- **Enemy Types:**
  - **Lurker** (Patrol behavior, 50 speed, 100 detection range)
  - **Chaser** (Chase behavior, 80 speed, 150 detection range)
- **Puzzle Types:**
  - Math puzzles (3rd-5th grade, 24 questions)
  - Word puzzles (unscramble 10 words)
  - Pattern puzzles (number sequences)
- **Item System:**
  - Batteries (üîã)
  - Colored keys (üî¥üîµüü°)
  - Upgrades (permanent meta-progression)

### Educational Value
- Grade-appropriate math (addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, order of operations)
- Vocabulary (word unscrambling)
- Pattern recognition (number sequences)
- Problem-solving under pressure
- Resource management

### Progression System
- **Per-Run Progress:**
  - Rooms explored
  - Puzzles solved
  - Items collected
  - Escape success/failure
- **Meta Progression (Upgrades with Memory Fragments):**
  - Longer Battery (+30s, 100 fragments)
  - Faster Movement (+10% speed, 150 fragments)
  - Bigger Backpack (+2 slots, 200 fragments)
  - Extra Health (+1 HP, 250 fragments)
  - Brighter Light (+25% radius, 300 fragments)
- **Reward Calculation:**
  - +5 fragments per room explored
  - +10 fragments per puzzle solved
  - +50 fragments for successful escape

### Atmosphere & Theme
- Dark, atmospheric tension (not gory/violent)
- Mystery narrative (uncover school secrets)
